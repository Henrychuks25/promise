Our Outstanding Alumni

Our aim is to give IJA alumni the opportunity to keep in touch with the developments of the School, attend IJA events and keep up to date with news.

We are committed to keeping in contact with as many alumni as possible and will always be pleased to hear from you.

The Alumni Association produce an annual newsletter which is published and sent to every IJA alumni with whom we are in touch. If you are not receiving a newsletter and would like to, please click here to securely update your contact details. Alternatively you can contact the Alumni Office directly on alumni@ijaasaba.com to pass the details on.

For those of you who haven't visited Infant Jesus Academy recently, why not come back and see us. You can always be sure of a warm welcome.
